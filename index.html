Auto Fair

Fight the Rickshasur!! Tired of paying extra on meter? Checkout Auto Fair - a free app to check the fare and distance to commute to your desired location. It also assists you with directions and road by road navigation.

Estimate Fare

Get the fare and distance to reach your destination based on shortest route available by simply filling up the fields using auto complete feature.

Maps Integration

Google Maps integration to assist you with road by road navigation and also to get the time to travel based on real time traffic conditions.

Multi City Support

Fare estimation based on your location's minimum fare, minimum distance and fare per km. Currently supporting Bengaluru, Chennai, Mumbai and Delhi.
